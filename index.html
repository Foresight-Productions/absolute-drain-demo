What We Do

Complete Drain & Septic Solutions

From routine maintenance to emergency repairs, we handle every aspect of your drain and septic system with precision and care.

Drain Cleaning

Stubborn clogs don't stand a chance. We use professional-grade equipment to clear any drain — kitchen, bathroom, floor, or main line.

Septic Pumping

Regular pumping keeps your septic system healthy. We recommend service every 3-5 years to prevent costly failures and backups.

Septic Installation

New construction or replacement — we design and install complete septic systems that meet all local codes and last for decades.

Sewer Line Repair

Broken, collapsed, or root-damaged sewer lines repaired right. We offer both traditional and trenchless repair options.

Grease Trap Cleaning

Keep your commercial kitchen compliant and flowing. Regular grease trap service prevents violations and plumbing emergencies.

Video Inspection

See exactly what's happening in your pipes. Our camera inspection technology pinpoints problems without digging up your yard.

Our Story

A Family Business Built on Trust

Absolute Drain & Septic, Inc. is the result of a father-and-son team coming together in 2008 to combine decades of hands-on experience into one company the Yakima Valley could count on.

What started as two independent operations became one unified team — driven by the belief that honest work, fair pricing, and showing up when you say you will goes a long way. That philosophy hasn't changed.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted names in drain and septic service across the Yakima Valley. Every job we take on is personal — because when your name is on the truck, reputation is everything.
